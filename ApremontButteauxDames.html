Apremont Butte aux Dames

This area, very popular to walkers due to its proximity of the parking lot of La Buvette des Brigands, has been referenced by climbers only in the recent years. Most of the problems are on the South hill next to La Route des Dames, where three circuits (orange, blue and black) are waiting to be officialized. Other problems, on sparse boulders along the North hill (107m on the IGN map) have also been opened. The access is rather simple from Le Chalet de la Caverne des Brigands. For the South hill, traverse to the right of La Route de Marie-Thérèse and for the North hill, just go up the slope above the parking lot.
