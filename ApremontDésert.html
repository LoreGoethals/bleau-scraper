Apremont Désert

This quiet area with three circuits has become a little more popular since new problems have been opened in 2014. Most of them are around Le Pilier du Désert in the lower part (and sometimes long-drying) of the area. The blue circuit, on the crest, is much quicker practicable. Access : from Le Bas-Cuvier parking lot (Apremont side), follow La Route de Clair-Bois southward, go straight on after Le Carrefour de Clair-Bois for 200m and go left in the forest for about 50m to the opposite of La Route des Chouettes.
