Rocher des Souris

From the parking lot of La Croix Saint-Jérôme, follow Le Chemin de la Vallée Close for 300m. The small sector of Rocher des Souris is just before the Chemin de la Plaine de Jean des Vignes (the big boulders of "Yogi" and "Extraction Terrestre" can easily be seen on the right).
