Mont Simonet

This small sector, located to the North of the classical sector of L'Éléphant, is composed of generally non exposed boulders around the hill of Mont Simonet. The simplest access consists in parking the car 200m after the parking lot of L'Éléphant, to follow La Route du Bois d'Hyver, and to turn right at La Route de la Chapelle à Larchant. The big boulder of La Citadelle can be found on the right and 200m further the one of Bidule. From that one, follow the blue path up the slope to the right to arrive to the main sector.
