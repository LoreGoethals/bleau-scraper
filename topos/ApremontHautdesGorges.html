Apremont Haut des Gorges

This small sector is in fact the North-East part of the classical area of Les Gorges d'Apremont. It is also an easy access to Le Vallon de la Solitude. Caution : due to the fragility of the sandstone in this shaded area, dry conditions are required to climb there. Access : from Égoïste, go up the thalweg for 100m up to a fork, take a path on the right for 100m, and go up the hill to the left to find the problems (35 to 44) of the salmon circuit in a shaded area. Other boulders can be found on the right side of the thalweg, towards Le Vallon de la Solitude.
