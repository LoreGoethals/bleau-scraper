Rocher de la Salamandre Est

This is the East part of the area of La Salamandre, a 3km long area whose classical problems are accessible from Le Carrefour d'Occident. However, new boulders have been opened in 2014-2015 at the very East, completing the few already existing problems. For the access, follow the D152 for 2km from Le Carrefour de l'Obélisque and park the car at the viaduct. Take the path for 200m and climb the steps : the first boulders are just above and the other ones, located on the South side, can be reached by following the path of the aqueduct and going up the slope.
